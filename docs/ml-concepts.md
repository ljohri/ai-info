## Reinforcement learning basics (DeepMind, 2015)

David Silver’s **UCL/DeepMind** course is the long-standing introduction many people still use; it’s **well over ten years old** in origin and remains a common reference for MDPs, value functions, and policy ideas.

- **Lecture 1 — Introduction to reinforcement learning** (starting point for the full course): [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2pWv7GOvuf0)  
- **Full course (playlist):** [DeepMind × UCL — Introduction to Reinforcement Learning (2015)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLqYmG7hTraZDM-OYHWgPebj2MfCFzFObQ)

## AlphaGo (full documentary, ~1 hour)

The well-known **AlphaGo** film (about 90 minutes) documents DeepMind’s system playing Go, including the match against Lee Sedol. A standard place to watch the **full** documentary is on **Google DeepMind’s YouTube channel**:

- [**AlphaGo — The Movie** (full award-winning documentary)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WXuK6gekU1Y) — *runtime ~1h 30m; use “official” / channel uploads to avoid re-uploads if you care about attribution.*
